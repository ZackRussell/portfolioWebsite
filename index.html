About

I turn raw data into
things that matter.

I'm a data analyst and manager based in Tulsa, Oklahoma, with 8 years of experience building the analytics systems that product and marketing teams rely on every day.

My work spans the full data lifecycle: architecting ETL pipelines, building executive dashboards, launching product analytics platforms across mobile app and desktop, and connecting disparate data sources into clean, reliable infrastructure.

I've done most of this work in the veterinary SaaS space, supporting both B2B and B2C analytics across enterprise clients and individual end users. This includes subscription funnel analysis, abandon rate tracking, and marketing site analytics across mobile app and desktop experiences. I'm comfortable owning data strategy end-to-end and translating complex findings into decisions non-technical stakeholders can act on.

Outside of data, I have a serious interest in film and the humanities, and I think the best analysts are people who can tell a story, not just build a query.
