Verdict
Assessment
Rising fast
The Critics Choice has surged from 20% accuracy in the 1990s to 83% in the 2020s, the joint-highest recent rate of any show. This likely reflects the CCA's expanded and increasingly diverse voting body aligning more closely with modern Academy tastes.
Recency matters here. The Critics Choice is a much stronger signal today than its historical average suggests. Weight it heavily in the current era.
