Verdict
Assessment
Steady but not dominant
The SAG ensemble award is voted on by actors, the largest bloc in the Academy, which gives it real predictive weight at 47% overall. It has been consistently in the 40-50% range across every decade it has existed, showing no signs of the decline seen in GG or BAFTA.
SAG is most powerful in combination with guild awards. A SAG + DGA + PGA sweep is the strongest possible Oscar signal.
