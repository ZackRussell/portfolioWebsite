Verdict
Assessment
Declining bellwether
Once the most-watched Oscar predictor, the Golden Globes Drama category has lost predictive power as the Academy expanded its voting body. It peaked at 71% in the 1940s and has slid to 27% since 2015. It is still better than a coin flip, but no longer a reliable forecast.
Best used as a signal of the critical frontrunner rather than a forecast of the eventual Oscar winner. When GG Drama and PGA agree, pay close attention.
